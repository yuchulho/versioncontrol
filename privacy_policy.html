Privacy Policy for dumpQV

Effective Date: April 28, 2026

1. Introduction

dumpQV is a personal Android application designed to open and analyze dumpstate log files. This app does not collect or transmit any personal information.

2. Data Collection

  • dumpQV does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • The app does not request device permissions. Only log files explicitly chosen by the user are processed.

3. Data Usage

  • Selected log files are used solely within the app for viewing and analysis.
  • No data is transmitted outside the app or stored permanently.

4. Permissions

  • dumpQV does not require any permissions.
  • The app does not use an internet connection.

5. Third-Party Services

  • This app does not integrate with third-party services.
  • It does not include ads, analytics tools, or tracking features.
